About

I am a professor at Foundation Baptist College and the Church Administrator at Meadowlands Baptist Church in Edmonton, AB. In the college, I teach Bible exposition and church history courses, and in my day-to-day work at the church I do a lot of different things. I also have some computer science background from my undergrad education, so I’ve put together a number of online projects.

My wife Meg teaches private violin lessons, when she isn’t busy taking care of our daughter. Some of the things we like to do together include reading, spending time with friends, and walking down to the neighborhood park with our little girl.

Education

  • Master of Divinity (2011). Seminary and Graduate School of Religion, Bob Jones University, Greenville, SC.
  • Bachelor of Arts (2006). School of Religion, Bob Jones University, Greenville, SC.
