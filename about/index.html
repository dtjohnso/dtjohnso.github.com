About

This website is my home for linking together all my various online interests. I have enjoyed teaching Bible exposition, theology, and church history courses at Foundation Baptist College in Edmonton, AB. I also have some computer science background from my undergrad education, so I’ve put together a number of online projects. Every once in a while, I even write blog posts here about theology, exegesis, and tech—but not too often yet, so I probably don’t actually have an audience yet besides you!

My wife Meg teaches private violin lessons. We like to read, spend time with friends, and take walks in the beautiful Edmonton river valley.

Education

  • Master of Divinity (2011). Seminary and Graduate School of Religion, Bob Jones University, Greenville, SC.
  • Bachelor of Arts (2006). School of Religion, Bob Jones University, Greenville, SC.
    Major: Bible (School of Religion)
    Minor: Information Systems Management (College of Arts and Sciences, Division of Mathematical Science, Department of Computer Science)
